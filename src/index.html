PROFESSIONAL POKER CAREER

I had a passion for gaming from a young age and after i graduated college i spent 4 years competing as a professional poker player.Â 

Becoming a professional in the world of poker was the toughest journey of my life, i had to build strong work ethic and consistency, make peace with the ups and downs of a highly competitive environment and embrace failure as a tool for learning and growth.

With a combination of passion and consistent 10+ hr daily practice, i managed to achieve few milestones: becoming one of the top 45man SNG players in 2014/15 era and achieving one of the highest winrates in Spin'n'go format.

Beacuse of my deep mathematical understanding of 45man SNG format , i was able to keep very high winrate with combination of mass-multitabling: being able to play up to 30 tables at the same time, which translates to playing up to 240 opponents simultaniously.

As time went by and global technology started drastically improving, i had to come to peace with the fact that deep neural network and AI self-learning technology will soon take over the world of online poker, so i decided to do what made the most sense: "If you cant beat them, join them". And thats how my journey into web-development started.
